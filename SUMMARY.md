## ğŸ’¡ Unique Selling Points

### 1. Source Buyout Innovation
**Problem**: When you repay the original lender but keep the loan active with the borrower.
**Solution**: One-click conversion from "Borrowed" to "Self-Funded" without affecting borrower terms.

### 2. Visual Capital Tracking
**Problem**: Hard to distinguish between different transaction types.
**Solution**: Color-coded cards (Blue=Cash, Green=Bank) with clear fund source tags.

### 3. Real-Time Interest Accrual
**Problem**: Manual interest calculation is error-prone.
**Solution**: Automatic daily calculation with live dashboard updates.

### 4. Smart Payment Reminders
**Problem**: Missing payment collection dates.
**Solution**: Three-tier notification system (T-3, T-0, T+1) with customizable timing.

### 5. Professional Data Export
**Problem**: Need to share data with accountants/partners.
**Solution**: One-tap Excel/CSV export with native sharing.
